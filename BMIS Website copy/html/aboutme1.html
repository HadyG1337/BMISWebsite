Background Information

I am Hayden Coltrane Gilmor-Flint. I am 19 years old and currently attend Gonzaga University. I love to skateboard, play basketball and lacrosse, and listen to music. Attending Gonzaga I chose to major in business and minor in biology. I hope to work in pharmaceuticals in the future, either on the marketing side or accounting side.

Early Life

I grew up in Fishers, Indiana. Though Indiana itself is not very exciting, I was always managed to have a good time there. I grew up around basketball my whole life and although Indiana University was there, I always rooted for Duke University.When I was a sophmore my family moved to Massachussets where I finsied by high school, eventually graduating from Westford Academy, where I played on the basketball and lacrosse teams.

Athletics

I always loved sports. From a young age basketball was all I ever wanted to play. Eventually however, I started playing lacrosse where I began to excel. When high school came around I was selected to be on varisty for both sports as a freshman. While I was a good basketball player throughout high school, lacrosse was actually my best sport. Playing lacrosse in both the midwest and east coast gave me great competition to play. I was all state 3 of my 4 years in high school and even picked up multiple college offers! However I decided I did not want to pursure sports in college and wanted to instead focus much more on my schooling and prepare for the next chapter of my life.
